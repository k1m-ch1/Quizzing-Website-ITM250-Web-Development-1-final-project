Figure Skating Jumps Quiz

Information

A quiz to discern between the different types of figure skating jumps. There are 6 main types of jumps, which are divided into two, toe-assisted jumps and edge jumps. I'm going to assume that the figure skater does a counter clockwise jump as that's most common. These are my heuristics to determine each type of jump:

    Toe assisted jumps:
  • Toe loop: uses the left toe pick to propel
  • Flip: uses the right toe pick to propel, left foot is skating on the back inside edge
  • Lutz: uses the right toe pick to propel, left foot is skating on the back outside edge. Usually a straight approach.
    Edge jumps:
  • Axel: jumps forwards, lands backwards
  • Salchow: legs are like, not crossed (idk... these ones are hard to see)
  • Loop: legs are crossed
