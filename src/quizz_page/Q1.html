JPEG vs PNG

Can you identify the image format just by looking?

💡 How to Play:
• Look at the background grid behind the image.
• If you see the checkerboard through the image, it has transparency (PNG).
• If the image has a solid background hiding the grid, it's likely a JPEG.
• Also look for compression "noise" (JPEG) vs clean edges (PNG).
